About Me

Cybersecurity, GRC & AI Governance Consultant & IT Project Manager

I am a technology and security consultant with over 25 years of experience spanning Banking, Healthcare, Education, Media, Telecom, Logistics, and Industrial IT environments. My career blends deep technical engineering with modern security governance, giving me the ability to operate at both strategic and execution layers.

I’ve led mission-critical initiatives including core banking migration (mainframe → Oracle), SOC/SIEM deployments using Splunk and Wazuh, enterprise-wide ISO 27001 implementations, HIPAA-aligned healthcare IT setups, and industrial-grade network rollouts across high-security environments.

With growing demand for AI accountability, I now specialize in AI Risk Management and ISO/IEC 42001:2023 AI Management Systems, helping founders build safe, defensible, and trustable AI systems.

I also author the Founder’s Risk Mirror series — practical, founder-friendly guidance for navigating AI risk, compliance, and governance.
